Publication Guidelines

Manuscript Preparation

Submit in APA 7th edition format. Include abstract, keywords, and up to 8,000 words. All submissions undergo double-blind peer review.

Research Ethics

All research must adhere to ethical guidelines. Include consent statements, conflict of interest declarations, and funding acknowledgments.

Review Process

Initial review within 2 weeks. Peer review completed within 6-8 weeks. Accepted articles published within 30 days.
